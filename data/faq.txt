# Cortex AI - Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Cortex AI?
Cortex AI is an intelligent conversational assistant powered by advanced language models and RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) technology. We help businesses provide instant, accurate support to their customers 24/7.

## What can Cortex AI help with?
- Answer customer questions instantly
- Provide product information and recommendations
- Handle common support inquiries
- Collect and qualify leads
- Schedule appointments
- And much more!

## How does Cortex AI work?
Cortex uses your business knowledge base to provide accurate, contextual responses. Simply upload your documents (FAQs, product guides, policies) and Cortex learns from them to assist your customers intelligently.

## Is Cortex AI available 24/7?
Yes! Cortex AI is available around the clock to assist your customers whenever they need help.

## Can I customize Cortex?
Absolutely! You can customize:
- Bot name and personality
- Color scheme and branding
- Knowledge base content
- Custom prompts and responses
- And more through the dashboard

## How do I get started?
1. Sign up for a Cortex account
2. Upload your business documents
3. Customize your bot settings
4. Add the widget to your website
5. Start helping customers instantly!
